About the Website

Anime Recommendations was created as a calm and welcoming space for anime lovers to find recommendations without feeling overwhelmed. The design focuses on accessibility, readability, and low-sensory comfort; using soft colors, clear navigation, and gentle animations.

The goal is to make anime discovery enjoyable for everyone, including people who are autistic, neurodivergent, or simply prefer a quieter digital environment.
